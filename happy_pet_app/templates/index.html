How to use app:
This application was created with pet parents in mind.
  • The parent dashboard gives you a place to store your information. It is recommended to have the most accessible email and phone number linked to the app in case your vet needs to reach you or send you important information.
  • The pet portal has a vast array of features designed to help you keep track of your pet's health. It has a compartmentalized layout that allows you to be as simplified or detailed as desired.
